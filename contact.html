Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

When are the mangoes in season?

Our mango season typically runs from March to July. Alphonso mangoes peak in April–May, while Kesar and Dasheri are available through June–July.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer special pricing for orders above 50kg. Please use the contact form above and select "Bulk Purchase" as the subject, and we'll reach out with a custom quote.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2–4 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at checkout for an additional charge. We ship pan-India.

What if my mangoes arrive damaged?

We stand behind our quality. If your order arrives damaged or unsatisfactory, please contact us within 24 hours with photos and we will arrange a replacement or full refund.

Are your mangoes organic / pesticide-free?

We follow natural farming practices and avoid synthetic pesticides. Our Alphonso and Kesar mangoes are certified under India's Organic Certification Scheme (NPOP).
